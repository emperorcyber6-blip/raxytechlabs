Cyber Decks

CONTROL • CREATIVITY • EXPLORATION

The Concept

Cyber decks are compact, custom-built personal computing systems designed for people who want full control over their digital tools. Unlike mass-produced laptops, they are modular, purpose-driven, and built around user workflow.

They reflect RAXY LABS’ belief in experimentation over consumption.

What Makes a Cyber Deck?

  • >> Rugged / Modular Form Factor
  • >> Physical Controls (Keys/Knobs)
  • >> Expandable Ports & Interfaces
  • >> Repairability & Ownership

Use Cases

Development & Coding Hardware Experimentation System Administration Portable Research Creative Work (Audio/Visuals)

Cyber Decks at RAXY LABS

We treat cyber decks as a future experimental initiative to explore alternative form factors.

  • Explore alternative computing
  • Combine hardware, software, and design
  • Build tools that encourage learning
"In the future, they may evolve into educational kits or specialized work tools. Today, they are a space for learning, testing, and pushing boundaries."
