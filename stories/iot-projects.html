IoT Experiments

CONNECTIVITY • DATA • HARDWARE

Overview

At RAXY LABS, IoT (Internet of Things) projects are internal research and prototype initiatives focused on connecting physical devices to software systems, data, and automation.

These projects are not client services. They are part of our R&D lab work, used to explore real-world system design, hardware–software integration, and intelligent environments.
