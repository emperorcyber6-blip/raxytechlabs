THE RAXY LABS STORY

RAXY TECH LABS, a rising African tech startup and software agency, was born in Kampala from curiosity, not capital.

It started with a young technologist who didn’t wait for permission, funding, or perfect conditions. What began as experimenting with code, systems, and hardware grew into a mindset: build first, learn first, improve relentlessly.

More Than a Traditional Agency

RAXY LABS is not a traditional agency — it is a lab. A place where software, systems, and ideas are tested, broken, and rebuilt stronger.

MULINDWA IBRAHIM (RAXY)

Founder — RAXY LABS

Mulindwa Ibrahim is a self-taught technologist driven by curiosity, experimentation, and long-term vision. He began by exploring software, systems, and hardware not as a business, but as a way to understand how technology really works.

Without funding or formal backing, he focused on building first — learning through trial, failure, and iteration. That mindset led to the creation of RAXY LABS — not as a typical company, but as a technology lab.

Key Milestones

  • >> Built and delivered real-world web platforms and payment integrations
  • >> Developed sell-ready systems such as POS and voting platforms
  • >> Created internal prototypes in smart systems and IoT
  • >> Planned long-term platforms like FX Journal and an Islamic digital platform
