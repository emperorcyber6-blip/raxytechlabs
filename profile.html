MULINDWA IBRAHIM (IBRIX RAXY)

Founder — RAXY LABS

Mulindwa Ibrahim is a self-taught technologist driven by curiosity, experimentation, and long-term vision. He began by exploring software, systems, and hardware not as a business, but as a way to understand how technology really works. Without funding or formal backing, he focused on building first — learning through trial, failure, and iteration.

That mindset led to the creation of RAXY LABS — not as a typical agency, but as a technology lab. A space to design, test, and refine systems that solve real problems while pushing into future tech.

Key Milestones:

  • Built and delivered real-world web platforms and payment integrations
  • Developed sell-ready systems such as POS and voting platforms
  • Created internal prototypes in smart systems and IoT
  • Planned long-term platforms like FX Journal and an Islamic digital platform

Mulindwa Ibrahim is not building for quick wins. He is engineering foundations — patiently, deliberately, and with scale in mind.
